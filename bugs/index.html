Get to Know Your Garden Heroes!

Sonechko has been in business since 1986, providing customers with many different varieties of insects. Recognized as a leader in the Beneficial Insect industry, we, at a grassroots level, provide natural alternatives to chemical pest control. Sonechko caters to the needs of both the large and small dealers, fulfilling all product requests.

Our eco-friendly approach to gardening is what makes our business outstanding. We provide a perfect alternative affordable garden activity for the whole family. Our goal is to promote the knowledge of the role of beneficial insects in sustaining the ecosystem and bring awareness about environment-friendly ways of gardening.
