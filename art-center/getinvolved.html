Member Benefits

  • Access to our Lending Library
  • Opportunity to exhibit artwork at the Art Center
  • 10% Discount on classes and in Paint Box Store
  • Members can attend the Open Studio on Modays from 12 to 3 for free at the Woodburn Art Center

What to Expect

We have no paid staff so we rely heavily on our volunteers to keep our doors open. We require a commitment of 3 hours per month in a wide variety of tasks, such as hosting, cleaning, taking sign-ups for classes & art shows and other jobs that need doing. Use of the computer and cash register is helpful, but training is provided as needed.
